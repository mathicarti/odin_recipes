Quiche

Description

A baked flan or tart with a savoury filling thickened with eggs, usually eaten cold.

Ingredients

  • 125g ham, chopped
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 1/2 cups (180g) grated tasty cheese
  • 1/3 cup (50g) Coles white self-raising flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups (375ml) milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200Â°C. Grease a 5 cup capacity pie dish.
  2. Combine ham, onion, cheese, flour in a medium bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Scatter over base of dish.
  3. Whisk eggs and milk together in a large jug and pour over ham mixture. Cook for about 40 minutes or until puffed and golden. Cool slightly. Serve warm or cold.
